Working at Depth Interactive

Building great software requires creativity, determination, technical expertise, focus, and collaboration, and we’ve worked to create an environment at Depth that encourages growth in all of these areas. Our office is a mix of open and private spaces, as at times you need the energy of the crowd, while oftentimes you need the ability to hone in on your craft without any distractions.

Our focus is on app and web creation for a host of different industries, we do full stack development, and specialize in application UI/UX design. Working at Depth gives you the opportunity to work with a talented, passionate team on a diverse set of projects, and there is always time to try something new, learn a new part of the process, and take ownership over what you are building.

company perks

Medical & Dental

We provide medical, dental, and vision insurance to every member of the team along with creating a low-stress environment with an emphasis on a healthy work-life balance.

401K Retirement

We offer a full 401k package with matching. It’s important every member of the team is rewarded for the work they do and better positioned for whatever path they choose in life.

Flexible Schedule

If you do your best work early in the morning or late at night, we’ll create a schedule that works for you. We believe in the benefits of remote work and our positions offer a mixture of office time and working from home.

Location

Our office is located in the heart of downtown Salt Lake City, close to a range of great lunch spots and only a few minute walk from the closest Trax station. Underground parking is also available and free to employees.

Learning

Learn new skills. Try out new technologies. At Depth, we're always looking to iterate and improve both individually and as a company.

People

We love what we do here at Depth. We respect each other, respect the work, and are always thinking about what we can design and build next.
